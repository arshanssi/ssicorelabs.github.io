Resilient Plant Intelligence for a Self-Reliant World

SSI Core Labs is building a new class of intelligent systems for lawns, gardens, greenhouses, and small farms. Our focus is simple: help people grow more with fewer resources while reducing dependence on fragile infrastructure.

We are developing technologies designed for local resilience, resource efficiency, and self-reliance, with a long-term vision for more sustainable homes and communities.
