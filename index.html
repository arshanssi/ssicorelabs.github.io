Giving Plants a Voice Through Intelligent Systems

SSI Core Labs builds sensing, control, and actuation platforms where plants, lawns, gardens, and trees communicate their needs through sensors, embedded intelligence, motors, and power electronics. The result is a closed-loop system that saves water, reduces fertilizer waste, improves crop yield, lowers disease risk, and supports long-term sustainability.

Vision

Plants, gardens, lawns, and trees should not stay silent.

SSI Core Labs envisions living systems that continuously express their needs through data, and engineered systems that respond with the right action at the right time. This is not just monitoring. It is bi-directional communication between nature and technology.

Listen

Capture soil, climate, and plant-health signals before visible damage appears.

Interpret

Fuse sensor inputs into meaningful plant stress, water demand, and health intelligence.

Act

Trigger motors, valves, pumps, and control systems to save resources and improve outcomes.

Technology Platform
